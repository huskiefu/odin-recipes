Egg Fried Rice

Egg Fried Rice is a fundamental dish synonymous with Chinese cuisine. There are many variations of this dish served in restaurants all around China and increasingly popular around the globe. There is no doubt egg fried rice is one of the basics and an entry drug to the world of chinese cuisine. Read below to learn the recipe and send in your own variations of egg fried rice to dongbei@china.com to get a chance to be featured on our instagram page!

Prepare the ingredients before hand for easy cooking. The actual cooking process will take 10-20 minutes if preparation has been done beforehand. If unable to find the ingredients in your local supermarket, feel free to substitute with any alternatives or buy them from your local Chinamart.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 servings of overnight rice
  • 2 eggs, beaten into the rice prior to cooking
  • 1tbsp minced garlic
  • 2tbsp cooking oil
  • 2tbsp Salt

Steps

  1. Saute minced garlic till fragrant
  2. Add in egg with stir fried till egg almost cooked
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Add diced spring onion and off flame. Serve
